About me

Darin was born in Los Angeles, California. He graduated from Los Angeles Mission College with an AA in Art in 2014 and graduated from UC Davis with a BA in Design in 2016.

He has been recognized for his “clean, smart design solutions with well-researched foundations”, “ability to transform personal stories into useful tools”, and “visual magic”.
